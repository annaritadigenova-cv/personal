1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) by your web browser when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember information about your visits and preferences.

Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser. Used for maintaining your browsing session.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored for an extended period. Remain on your device until expiration or manual deletion.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by our website directly.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services (Google, Meta, LinkedIn) integrated on our site.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • Understand how you use our website

8. How to Manage Cookies

8.1 Using Our Cookie Banner

The easiest way to manage cookies is using the cookie preference banner at the bottom of our website:

  • Click "Settings" to open the preferences modal
  • Toggle each category ON or OFF
  • Click "Save Preferences" to apply your choices
  • Click "Reject All" to disable non-essential cookies

8.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your web browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies and site permissions
  • Opera: Preferences → Advanced → Cookies
⚠️ Note: Blocking cookies may affect website functionality and your browsing experience. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking site features.

8.3 Third-Party Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Browser extension
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): Opt-out portal
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): Consumer choice
  • ePrivacy Directive: EU opt-out platform
